Eat, Drink and Enjoy!

Welcome to Drury Buildings, a unique venue right in the city center of Dublin, with a superb colour vibrancy from our street art wallpaper. Before it became the building with the beautiful mural it was a sewing factory and we are very proud to say that the building itself was earmarked for demolition when we took it on. However we had a different vision because sustainability is very important to us. Captured carbon in our built environment has to stay captured if we want to have sustainable cities. During our journey, first started in 2013, we developed a place with a perfect athmosphere where you and your friends can Eat, Drink and Enjoy! from a wide range of unique choices. Our dedicated team is working endless to combine multiple concepts to create the perfect Menu.
